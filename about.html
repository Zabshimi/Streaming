Streaming

What is streaming?

Streaming is a method of transmitting videos or audios over the internet using a device such as a computer without the hassle of downloading it.

Types of

Streaming Content

Gaming

The Gaming Video Content (GVC) industry includes related online media platforms, competitions, advertisers, video games and sponsors of video game players who stream and upload original created content online, amass public following, and receive advertising or sponsorship benefits

Movies

Movie is an electronic signal of moving graphics, pictures, or text used to combine a steady stream of images used for entertainment, education, or other uses

Music

WMusic is the art of arranging sounds in time through the elements of melody, harmony, rhythm, and timbre

Podcast

A podcast series usually features one or more recurring hosts engaged in a discussion about a particular topic or current event.

Platforms Used for
Streaming

  • Youtube

    YouTube is an American online video sharing and social media platform owned by Google. It is the second most visited website, with more than one billion monthly users who collectively watch more than one billion hours of videos each day

  • Twitch

    Twitch is an American video live streaming service that focuses on video game live streaming, including broadcasts of esports competitions.

  • Facebook

    Facebook is defined as an online social networking website where people can create profiles, share information such as photos and quotes about themselves, and respond or link to the information posted by others.

  • Netflix

    It offers subscription-based video on demand from a library of films and television series.

  • V Live

    sometimes referred to as V App, is a South Korean live video streaming service that allows celebrities based in the country to broadcast live videos such as live chat sessions with fans.

  • Spotify

    It is one of the world's largest music streaming service providers.

Characteristics of a Good Stream

- Informative
- Entertaining
- Consistent
- High quality
- True and Authentic
